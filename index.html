Apollo

We are building a new type of subnet in the Bittensor ecosystem; one where miners do not compete, but instead pool their powers to achieve greater things together. Our first project: to create a network which can perform highly-efficient generation of zero-knowledge proofs, scaling linearly in the amount of miners participating in the subnet - opening up usecases never before thought possible.

Apollo is currently live on the Bittensor testnet, at UID 115. You can check out the links below to learn more.
